Brett Silverman, blsilver@fas

MyrchMe will allow for its users to buy gifts for themselves and for friends based on a set of predetermined preferences, frequency and target price. MyrchMe seeks to make both shopping and gift-buying a social, yet automated experience, all the while balancing the joy of a well-timed surprise and the comfort of always getting what you asked for.
