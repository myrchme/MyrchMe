MyrchMe allows for its users to buy gifts for themselves and for friends based on a set of predetermined preferences, frequency and target price. MyrchMe seeks to make both shopping and gift-buying a social, yet automated experience, all the while balancing the joy of a well-timed surprise and the comfort of always getting what you asked for. Do you want to get started? All it takes is three easy steps:

Step 1
-> Sign up!

Step 2
-> Select preferences!

Step 3
-> Sit back, relax, and enjoy the gifts!
