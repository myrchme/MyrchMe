Required Fields

  • Category: Select one from the folowinglist
  • Title: Name of the product (max = 70 characters)
  • Description: Brief description of the product
  • Product ID: Your store's unique int or string indetifier for the product (max = 30 characters)
  • Condition: Either NEW, USED, or REFURB
  • Price: In xx.xx or xx format
  • Link: URL to the product
  • Image URL: URL to the image of the product

Optional Fields

  • ISBN: max = 13 characters
  • UPC: max = 12 characters
  • Brand: max = 50 characters
  • Color: Any string of your choosing (max = 20 characters)
  • Size: Choose: XS,S,M,L,XL,XXL
  • Gender: Choose: M or F
